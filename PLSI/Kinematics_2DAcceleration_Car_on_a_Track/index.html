Centripetal Acceleration Track Game

Follow a car around a closed track and connect steering, speed changes, velocity, tangential acceleration, and centripetal acceleration.

How to use

The car follows the center of the track automatically. Use the acceleration slider to make the car speed up, coast, or slow down. Watch the velocity arrow, total acceleration arrow, and dashed component arrows attached to the car.

The right panel shows the driver view: the road ahead bends in the direction of the upcoming turn, and the steering wheel turns in the same direction as the centripetal acceleration. Keyboard controls: press Space to play or pause, R to reset, S to step forward, and use the arrow keys on the acceleration slider to adjust speeding up or slowing down.
