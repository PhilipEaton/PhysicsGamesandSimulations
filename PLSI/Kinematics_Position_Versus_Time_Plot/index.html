Position vs. Time Slope Explorer

Explore how the slope of a position-versus-time graph represents velocity.

How to use

Choose a preset, generate a random graph, or create a custom cubic. Move the selected time to see the point and tangent line. The slope of the tangent line is the velocity at that instant.

Mouse and touch users can click or drag near the curve. Keyboard users can use the selected time slider, step buttons, or arrow keys when the graph has focus.
