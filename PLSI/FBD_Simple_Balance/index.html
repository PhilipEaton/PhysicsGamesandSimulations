FBD Balance Trainer

Practice balancing a free-body diagram by drawing the horizontal and vertical components of the missing force.

How to use

The blue arrows are the forces already acting on the block. Use the component dropdown to choose the horizontal or vertical component, then drag from the block to draw that part of the force needed to balance the diagram. A close answer snaps to the exact value.

Keyboard option: choose a component, then use the arrow keys to adjust the selected answer. Hold Shift for 5 N steps. Press Enter to check the selected component. Press R to reset the current attempt or Space for a new problem.

Controls

Component controls

Choose the component from the dropdown, then drag on the diagram or use the keyboard/numeric controls. When only one unfinished component remains, it is selected automatically.
