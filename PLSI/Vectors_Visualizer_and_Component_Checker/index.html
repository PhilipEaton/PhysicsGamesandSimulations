Vector Component Checker

Enter a vector using magnitude and direction, visualize it, calculate the horizontal and vertical components, and check your answer without immediately revealing the solution.

How to use

  1. Choose the direction vocabulary: compass directions or up/down/left/right.
  2. Enter the vector magnitude and direction.
  3. Use the diagram to visualize the vector immediately.
  4. Calculate the horizontal and vertical components yourself.
  5. Enter your components and check your answer. The calculated components stay hidden until your answers are close enough.
